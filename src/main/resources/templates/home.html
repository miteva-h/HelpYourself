Explore

You are feeling stressed and depressed and are looking for some help? Go check some of the latest scientific papers in the psychology field. Try to search throw them and find what is more relevant to your current situation. You can also search by some term in the title or sort them by date. And always remember, you are not alone in this.


Advice

So you are searching for more personal way to get help and learn how to deal with your stress, then take this quiz to see what is the main reason for your anger and depression and get some advice about how to cope whit that feeling. We do not save data about your answers so be free to answer then honestly.


Diary

You can use this online diary as a mark for you to check how are you getting better and how fast are you learning to cope with your feelings. Write the date and start writing about your day. You can always check up to see your progress and always remember to be proud of yourself, because every little step is better than yesterday.
